# Stage 14 — Quality Control

**PIC:** pm · **Gate out:** internal_qc

## Goal
Check work against the design before handover.

## Outputs
Punch list + minor-revision list. Internal QC clears the gate.

## Tech choice
Structured checklist (dimensions, finishing, doors/drawers, electrical, cleanliness, design match).

## AI / automation
Vision model drafts a punch list from QC photos (defects, misalignment); PM confirms.
